Our Mission - School the Unschooled

Dharna is a innovative educational platform that focuses on increasing the efficiency of both the teaching and the learning process, by imbibing innovation and latest technology that has immense potential in the field of education. Moreover, Dharna is an attempt to improve upon the teacher to student ratio by making learning on diverse topics more accessible.

With time, all aspects of our life is getting more and more dependent on technology, be it communication, transportation, commerce or any other important part of our life.

One area, technology has affected the least is education and learning. We are still stuck with old classroom method of teaching, where a single teacher strives hard to teach a whole batch or section of students. Eventhough, video tutorial applications and e-learning is getting more and more popular, it seems to be lacking the most important fact of learning i.e, recieveing and analysing learner feedback such as emotion, interest and attention of the student.

We strive to create a robust product that not only allows realtime feedback while e-learning but also, analyses the learner feedback at realtime and modifies teaching strategy on the fly depending on the learner. So, the learner not only benefits from having a dedicated teacher paying individual attention, but also frees up the teachers to decide the content and flow of a learning session and not worry about distractions and disturbance which might be prevalent in a classroom session.

Our mission is simple, provide a single teacher or teaching agent to each and every learner and bringforth a revolution where learning is automated as well.
